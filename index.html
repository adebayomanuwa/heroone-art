Musical Identity

More than just an artist.

Creator of Beats & Blues

Hero One started with Ghetto Gospel Reality Music, but the sound kept growing. What came next was Beats & Blues: a raw and soulful fusion of hip-hop, Afro-influences, Modern Classics, and multiple genres.
